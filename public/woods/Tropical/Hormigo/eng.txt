Platymiscium dimarphandrum
Hormigo wood, also known as Macacauba or Macawood, is a dense, exotic hardwood with a finely textured grain and a range of colors from red and orange to darker red and brown. 
Works and machines well with sharp hand and power tools, turns and glues well, and yields a high polish. Has a natural luster and a high degree of chatoyance often emerges after finish sanding. 
Used for Furniture, guitars, and other woodworking projects.

Jobillo wood, also known as Goncalo Alves or Tigerwood, is a durable, reddish-brown hardwood with dark streaks, prized for its beauty and used in furniture, flooring, and decorative items. It is Moderately hard and heavy. Very durable, resists decay, and has excellent weathering properties.  Generally not too difficult to work, though figured pieces with irregular grain can pose a challenge in planing and machining. Turns and finishes well.

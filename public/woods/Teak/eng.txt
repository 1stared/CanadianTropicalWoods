Teak (Tectona grandis) 
is a tropical hardwoodtree species in the family Lamiaceae. It is a large, deciduous tree that occurs in mixed hardwood forests. 
Teak wood has a leather-like smell when it is freshly milled and is particularly valued for its durability and water resistance. The wood is used for boat building, exterior construction, veneer, furniture, carving, turnings, and various small project
